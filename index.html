FAQ

Before you check out

When does Black Friday 2026 start for hosting? +

Most hosts open their sales the week of Nov 17 and keep them running through Cyber Monday (Dec 1). A handful — WPX and Hostinger among them — stretch their windows into the first week of December.

Are these coupon codes verified? +

Every code and price on this page is pulled straight from each merchant's official Black Friday landing page, and we recheck them daily while the sale window is open.

Which host offers the deepest discount? +

Hostinger, WebHostingPad and BigRock all clear the 90% mark on entry-level shared plans. If managed WordPress is the goal, WPEngine's five free months is the strongest offer on the list.

Should I renew now or wait? +

If your current plan renews before mid-January, grab an early renewal with the Black Friday coupon — most hosts will stack the extra term on top of your existing subscription rather than making you wait.
